Quick Start
New here? Here’s how DeskGuard works.
Use the map to find a free desk, point your camera to scan its QR code, and keep your session active with the timer controls.
1

Find a green desk

Green means free, red means occupied, yellow means away, and purple means abandoned.

2

Scan to check in

Tap a free desk on the map, choose your scan method, and check in securely.

3

Use the session controls

Pause when you step away, confirm when prompted, and check out when you leave.
